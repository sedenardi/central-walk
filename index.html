About

Durring the summer of 2014, I took advantage of NYC's summer weather and walked to and from work through the iconic Central Park. Using my Android phone I recorded over 40 of the paths I took. I tried to take different paths each time so I could explore the park more completely, though there are a few overlapping paths.

  • Use the controls to toggle the playback and adjust speed.
  • Choose which matric with which to shade each path.
  • Hover over each path to see detailed stats.
  • Click on the icons to see details about that attraction.
